Who am I?

Honestly I'm not that important, just a student and a programmer. My work probably won't make me famous or change the world, but I hope that in my little corner of the earth, I can inspire someone or make just a few lives better. I was always told to leave things better than I found them and I hope to do that with my career. In the meantime, I hope to inspire you with my simple creations.
